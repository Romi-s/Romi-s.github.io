About Me

Control engineer by training and machine learning practitioner by choice. I'm most comfortable working at the intersection of control, perception, and learning.

I recently completed an MSc in Automation and Control Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, with work spanning both hands-on robotics and end-to-end ML development — from deploying model predictive control on real robotic hardware to building deep learning pipelines and agentic AI systems.

My thesis combined these threads in an open-vocabulary perception system for robotic manipulation, using foundation models to produce structured scene graphs that enable a robot to understand not only what it sees, but how objects relate to one another in space.
